Fried Matzah

Fried Matzah or Matzo brei is a delicious breakfast treat of Matzo that is soaked in a batter of milk and egg and then pan fried. It is often served with fruit preserves.

Ingredients

  • Matzo (whole wheat holds up best to soaking)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • Strawberry preserves

Steps

  1. Break piece of matzah in half and put it in colander. Lightly run hot water over matzah to soften.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Whisk togther 2 eggs and 1 cup of milk. Pour into a shallow flat container, slightly larger than the pieces of matzah.
  4. Take the matzah out of the colander and place in the egg-milk mixture. Soak for 30 seconds to a minute.
  5. Take a new piece of matzah and soften it with water.
  6. Put the matzah in the pan and fry until golden brown.
  7. Repeat the process as desired until you've fried as much matzah as you'd like.
  8. Serve with a dollop of strawberry preserves.
